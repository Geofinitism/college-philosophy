## Mission

The College of Philosophy is where Geofinitism speaks directly to the Western philosophical tradition. It does not reject that tradition — it reabsorbs it. The essays here show how Geofinitism's measurement-first, finite-symbolic approach dissolves or reframes the classical problems of philosophy of mathematics, logic, ontology, and epistemology: not by denying the questions, but by recognising that the questions were symptoms of treating finite symbolic instruments as if they were windows onto an infinite, Platonic terrain.

Where the College of Finite Symbolic Mechanics develops the formal apparatus, and the College of Language Dynamics develops the dynamical theory of meaning, the College of Philosophy asks: what kind of world is one in which all knowledge is measurement-conditioned? What replaces Platonic realism? What becomes of truth, proof, and existence when instruments are included in the frame?

**Central questions:**
- What is admissibility, and why does it replace correspondence as the standard for symbolic commitment?
- How does Geofinitism dissolve classical paradoxes (Russell, Zeno, Liar, Banach–Tarski) rather than solve them?
- What does Gödel's incompleteness theorems mean when incompleteness is a *feature* of finite measurement, not a deep truth about reality?
- What is ∼Truth in a measurement-first framework — and what does it take for a symbolic trajectory to be stable?
- How should we reason under conditions of principled uncertainty, and what does INDETERMINATE do philosophically?

## §5 — The Geofinite Dissolution Method

A recurring pattern across the philosophical essays: classical problems are not solved but dissolved by revealing them as symptoms.

```
Classical problem presented (e.g., Russell's Paradox, Zeno's Paradoxes)
        ↓
Identify the hidden assumption: that symbolic systems can operate without
finite grounding — that symbols are weightless, costless, and perfectly precise
        ↓
Apply Geofinite measurement-first constraint:
All symbols are finite, have cost, uncertainty, and provenance
        ↓
The paradox dissolves: it was a boundary effect of pushing a compression
system beyond its representational capacity
        ↓
Result: not a solution (which would accept the problem's framing)
but a re-diagnosis (which reframes the problem as symptom)
```
